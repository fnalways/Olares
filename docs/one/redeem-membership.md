# Redeem Olares Space membership <Badge type="tip" text="Kickstarter Backers Only" />

If you backed Olares One on Kickstarter, your package includes an "Olares Space Membership | Basic" card. This complimentary membership provides enhanced cloud services and is linked to the email address used for your Kickstarter order.

Follow the steps on this page to redeem your membership and unlock the full potential of [Olares Space](../space/index.md).

## Redemption steps

1. Go to https://space.olares.com in web browser.
2. Use the LarePass app to scan the QR code on screen to log in.
3. Go to **Usage & billing** > **Account email**, and then click **Bind** to enter the email address used for your Kickstarter order. 

    When the verification is completed, your Basic membership is activated immediately.
